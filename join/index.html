Open call

Join the Team

Unscriptulous is a long-form narrative ensemble formed in May 2025, currently developing our second production. We're looking for new members who love imaginative, character-led storytelling and thrive in a creative, expressive team.

Experience & Training

  • Completion of Free Association Level 3 (or equivalent) is the minimum — ideally you'll have progressed beyond this, or have comparable experience at another top improv school.
  • Experience in narrative improv — through team performance or the Free Association Narrative Course (or similar at another long-form school) — is required.
  • Experience performing with teams in other formats is a plus.

Who We're Looking For

Adventurous, generous performers comfortable taking creative risks together. We value openness, empathy, and the ability to connect with both fellow players and audiences through honesty and play.

Commitment

  • Rehearsal blocks of 3–4 months, roughly every other week on average, with regular performances in London.
  • A shared commitment to creating excellent, high-quality improv.
  • Willingness to take on administrative or organisational roles — from show logistics to production or marketing — to help the team operate professionally.
  • We're looking for players who can prioritise this ensemble and are not spread too thin across other teams, contributing consistently and with focus.

How to Apply

Send a short note about your experience and why you'd like to join to hello@unscriptulous.com. Please include:

  • Your current teams or projects.
  • Your improv training and schools attended.
  • Links to your social media handles (Instagram, TikTok, etc.).
  • Administrative tasks you could commit to and any professional or creative skills (design, production, admin, marketing).
hello@unscriptulous.com

Application Process

We're keeping the process fluid. Next steps will depend on the response we get — we want everyone to have a fair, supportive chance to play with us.
