Join Unscriptulous

We’re looking for new ensemble members who love imaginative, character-led storytelling and thrive in a team that’s creative, expressive, and unafraid to explore the edges of humour and emotion.

← Back to Home

Experience & Training

  • Completion of Free Association Level 3 (or equivalent training) is the minimum we expect — ideally, applicants will have progressed beyond this or have equivalent experience at another top improv school.
  • Experience in narrative improv — either through team performance or the Free Association Narrative Course (or similar at another long-form school) — is required.
  • Experience performing with teams in other improv formats is a plus.

Who We’re Looking For

  • Adventurous, generous performers who are comfortable taking creative risks together. We value openness, empathy, and the ability to connect with both fellow players and audiences through honesty and play.
  • Gen Z improvisers: Passionate about shaping the future of narrative improv in London, bringing energy, perspective, and a hunger to experiment.
  • Underrepresented voices: We especially encourage applications from FLINTA*, LGBTQ+ and ethnic minority background players. We’re committed to building a team that reflects the full diversity of the communities we perform for.

Commitment

  • Rehearsals take place every other week, with regular performances in London.
  • A shared commitment to creating excellent, high-quality improv.
  • A willingness to take on and complete necessary administrative or organisational roles — from show logistics to production or marketing — to help the team operate professionally.
  • We’re looking for players who can prioritise this ensemble alongside their other projects — ideally those not spread across too many teams — so they can contribute consistently and with focus.

How to Apply

If this sounds like you, send a short note about your experience and why you’d like to join to hello@unscriptulous.com. Please include:

  • Your current teams or projects.
  • Your improv training and schools attended.
  • Links to your social media handles (Instagram, TikTok, etc.).
  • Any professional or creative skills (e.g., design, production, admin, marketing) that could help the team run smoothly.
