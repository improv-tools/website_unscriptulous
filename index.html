Unscriptulous

Long-form narrative improv

Seven players. One story. Always Unscriptulous.

Founded in 2025, Unscriptulous are a London-based ensemble: Dan, Eki, Ella, Jack, Kat, Kristina and Martin. They perform cinematic, character-driven long-form narratives built entirely in the moment — weaving comedy, emotion, and story without a script. Our players have been trained at top schools and by world-leading coaches.

A performance approach that uses minimal input (often one suggestion) to create multiple scenes over a longer set. Scenes may be connected or stand alone; formats vary from single-story pieces to looser shows that explore themes, characters, or ideas across the set.

A style of improv that prioritises story: cause-and-effect, character journeys, and plot arcs build toward a cohesive beginning, middle, and end — like watching an unscripted play unfold.

Unscripted performance made up in the moment. Dialogue, action, and characters are created live by the players in collaboration with the audience’s energy and suggestions.
