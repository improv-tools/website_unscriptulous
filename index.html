Disaster Movie

A long-form improvised blockbuster where the audience gives the spark and the cast creates the catastrophe. Every performance is a brand-new epic full of danger, comedy, panic and impossible choices.

  • One continuous story built live by all eight performers.
  • Past disasters have included floods, the world burning and sharknados.
  • Big characters, cinematic stakes and a totally fresh ending every time.

About the group

Long-form. Narrative. Improv.

Long form

One suggestion becomes an extended performance, letting scenes, relationships and ideas grow across a full set rather than a series of short games.

Narrative

Story comes first: character journeys, cause and effect, escalating stakes and satisfying plot turns build toward a clear beginning, middle and end.

Improv

Nothing is scripted. Dialogue, action and characters are created live in the room, shaped in real time by the cast and the audience suggestion.
